Correlation of selected crypto companies with Axie Infinity game tokens

Introduction

Canaan Inc and Riot Blockchain are two companies listed on the Nasdaq exchange, whose activities are related to Bitcoin mining. Canaan is a producer of specialized devices for high performance computing - called ASIC (application specific integrated circuit), used, among others, for calculations securing the Bitcoin network. Riot Blockchain is a mining company supporting Bitcoin blockchain by mining and securing networks on a large scale in the United States. The company is focused on reducing greenhouse gas emissions and the widest possible use of renewable energy sources. Coinbase is a Nasdaq-listed company that provides cryptocurrency exchange services, the so-called CEX (centralized exchange). Axie Infinity is an online video game based on NFT (Non-Fungible Tokens) tokens created by the Vietnamese studio Sky Mavis, which uses cryptocurrencies based on the Ethereum protocol called AXS and SLP.

Objective

The aim of this article is to compare the relationships between selected entities and Axie Infinity game token. It is also an attempt to determine how strongly correlated the selected entities in the cryptocurrency industry are with each other in terms of daily percentage changes of their rates of return. To seek an answer to the question of whether these entities behaved similarly in the analyzed period.

Methodology

A target group was selected for the research, consisting of three companies listed on classic stock exchanges and a single token listed mainly on centralized cryptocurrency exchanges (CEX) as well as on automated cryptocurrency exchanges AMM, such as Uniswap.

The time period has been set for the dates between 04/04/2021 and 14/07/2021. This period includes both the uptrend from the beginning of the year and the downtrend from the second half of 2021.

The basic data of the subjects participating in the research are presented in the table below.

In USD billion Activities Market valuation
Coinbase Centralized crypto exchange 46.90
Canaan Bitcoin mining 0.98
Riot Blockchain Bitcoin mining 2.36
Axie Infinity Crypto game 2.30

Then, in order to automate the analysis process, a Python program was written consisting of several functions, using known data science ecosystem libraries such as numpy, pandas, scipy, matplotlib and others. The aim of the software was to collect data on the stock quotes of the analyzed entities from the Internet, cleaning, processing and presentation. In this way, the entire process becomes repeatable, and it is possible to update it and run it again in successive periods of time.

One of the first problems encountered was obtaining uniform data. During the research, it turned out that Axie Infinity game tokens are not in the stooq.com database, so it was decided that the data would be obtained from coingecko.com. Due to the separate methods of data presentation between stooq.com and coingecko.com, additional functions of the program had been written in order to standardize the available data.

After the data was obtained, the Pearson correlation coefficient matrix was computed. That is, a matrix whose elements are the values of correlation coefficients for the appropriate pairs of variables, which in this case are the daily rates of return for individual entities. The computed correlation matrix was presented by means of a heatmap.


The correlation coefficient r is between <-1.1>, where values -1 and 1 indicate perfect correlation and 0 no correlation.

The strength of the correlation for the absolute value | r | (dependency level)

  • <0.2 - none
  • 0.2 - 0.4 - weak
  • 0.4 - 0.7 - moderate
  • 0.7 - 0.9 - quite strong
  • 0.9 - very strong

Results

The research results show that in the analyzed period there was no strong relationship between the daily rates of return of selected entities.

The heat map shows that there is a moderate correlation between Riot Blockchain and Canaan Inc, which is understandable given the complementary scope of activities.


Fig. 2. Heat map of the matrix of correlation coefficients of selected entities in the cryptoassets industry Source: Own study


On the other hand, the analysis showed no correlation between Canaan Inc, Riot Blockchain, Coinbase, and Axie Infinity game tokens, which is a completely separate segment of the cryptoasset industry. Coinbase’s correlation with other entities also remains weak during the period considered. Regression analysis for the companies with the highest degree of correlation, Canaan Inc and Riot Blockchain, also shows only a moderate relationship between the companies, with high data dispersion.


Fig 3. Linear regression of Canaan Inc and Riot Blockchain companies distinguished by the highest level of correlation among the tested entities Source: Own study


Conclusion

The research results show that there are entities whose daily rates of return are not correlated with each other. So despite the general view that the entire crypto market is highly correlated with each other, it seems to be possible to create a portfolio with significant diversification only in the area of the crypto industry.

Diversification into different areas of the crypto asset industry is key to a sustainable portfolio
