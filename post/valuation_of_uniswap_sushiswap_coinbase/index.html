Comparable valuation of cryptocurrency exchanges Uniswap, Sushiswap and Coinbase

Introduction

Coinbase is a Nasdaq listed company operating one of the largest centralized exchanges in the crypto industry. Unlike Coinbase, Uniswap and Sushiswap are not enterprises in the classic sense of the Commercial Companies Code.

Both Uniswap and Sushiswap are protocols that provide liquidity and allow automated trading of ERC-20 tokens with Ethereum. In other words, they are decentralized automatic exchanges operating on the Ethereum blockchain, abbreviated as DEX - (decentralized exchange). The source code of both exchanges is open source, anyone who knows how to program can review it or analyze it in terms of security. In fact, the Sushiswap exchange is a fork of the Uniswap exchange. In other words, Sushiswap is based on the Uniswap source code. Both stock exchanges are currently the foundation of decentralized finance - DeFi - Decentralized Finance.

What is the valuation of these three exchanges?

Purpose

The value of an organization that is publically traded is provided by the market itself. However, the purpose of this article is not to evaluate, but to determine which of the above mentioned organizations may potentially be underestimated by the market.

Methodology

For this purpose, we collected market valuations of the three exchanges and their revenues. We tried to obtain the latest revenue data where possible. Coinbase released its latest quarterly report for March 2021, which reports revenues of over $ 1.8 billion. To maintain a more conservative analysis profile, we decided to assume revenues for the end of 2020 of USD 1.2 billion. For Uniswap and Sushiswap, we decided to take the revenues for the last 30 days, extrapolated to 12 months. In this way, we also maintain a conservative approach by avoiding the extraordinary revenues from the beginning of the year, when the market was still in a strong bull phase.

All this data was loaded into a software written in Python using the Pandas and Matplotlib libraries as shown in the figure below.

Indicators

Based on those numbers, the market cap to sales ratio was calculated for each of the exchanges. This is roughly similar to the core P / S market value ratio used in the company analysis, however, since protocols do not own shares, the per-share value cannot be used here, as is the case for the P / S ratio. The obtained results were illustrated on a graph made with the Python Matplotlib library.

Results


Fig 2. Market cap comparison of the analyzed cryptocurrency exchanges Source: Own study


As shown in the figure above, Coinbase currently has the highest valuation in relation to revenues. Both dex protocols have a lower valuation. A characteristic feature is the significantly lower value of the market cap to sales ratio for the Uniswap exchange in the situation of a comparable level of sales adopted for the analysis. In this case, the ratio of market capitalization of Coinbase to its revenues is more than twice as high as the ratio for the Uniswap exchange.

Conclusion

The presented analysis shows that the Sushiswap exchange remains the most undervalued. There could be many reasons for this. The undervaluation of Sushiswap versus Uniswap may be due to Uniswap’s faster implementation of new innovative protocols. It may also be the result of network effects and the fact that Uniswap was the first on the market. On the other hand, the reason for the lower valuation of both Dexes in relation to the Coinbase exchange may be the more difficult access to the crypto market and DeFi products for institutional investors.

It could be said, that if Sushiswap maintains the pace of implementing innovative solutions, it is worth adding Sushi tokens to your portfolio.
